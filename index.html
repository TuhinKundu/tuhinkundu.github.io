Tuhin Kundu

Hi, I'm Tuhin, currently a masters student in Computer Science at the University of Illinois at Chicago, where I'm advised by Prof. Cornelia Caragea.

My research interests include deep learning applications in natural language processing (NLP) and computer vision.

I've previously interned at:

  • IIIT Delhi: deep learning, biomedical imaging
  • ISI Kolkata: deep learning
  • Jadavpur University: computer vision, machine learning
  • IIT Kanpur: systems programming

My current research focus is on transformer-based architectures in deep learning for downstream NLP tasks such as named entity recognition, classification, question answering amongst others.

I also hold side interests in transfer learning, multi-task learning, generative models and adversarial learning.

I intend to graduate Spring 2021!
